Welcome to Cryptid Corner!

This website is dedicated to all things cryptids and the supernatural. Cryptids can be defined as the mysterious creatures of folktales and legends, such as Bigfoot or the Loch Ness Monster. This website is not here to either prove or disprove the existence of these creatures, but to simply provide information on them. While cryptids are found all around the world, this site will mostly focus on North American cryptids for now (with a few exceptions).
